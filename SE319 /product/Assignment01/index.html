Engineering Disciplines

This catalog serves as a description of two distinct yet similar computing disciplines: Software Engineering, Computer Engineering, Cybersecurity Engineering and Electrical Engineering.

Software Engineering

Software engineering is the systematic application of principles, techniques, and tools to design, develop, test, and maintain software systems.

Computer Engineering

Computer engineering involves the design, development, and integration of hardware and software components to create efficient computing systems.

Cybersecurity Engineering

Cybersecurity engineering involves the development and implementation of measures to protect digital systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and breaches.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering focuses on the study, design, and application of electrical systems, devices, and technologies, encompassing areas such as power generation, electronics, and telecommunications.
