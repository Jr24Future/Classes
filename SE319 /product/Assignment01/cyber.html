Cyber Security

Cybersecurity involves safeguarding digital systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, cyberattacks, and breaches. It encompasses various practices such as encryption, intrusion detection, and vulnerability assessment. As technology advances, cybersecurity becomes increasingly vital in protecting individuals, organizations, and nations from cyber threats. By employing techniques rooted in computer science and information technology, cybersecurity professionals play a critical role in maintaining the integrity and confidentiality of digital assets.
