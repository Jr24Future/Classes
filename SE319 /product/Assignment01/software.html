Software Engineer

Software engineering is the systematic approach to the design, development, testing, and maintenance of software systems. It encompasses various methodologies and practices aimed at creating reliable, efficient, and scalable software solutions. With a focus on both technical excellence and collaboration, software engineering seeks to address complex problems by applying principles of computer science, project management, and quality assurance.
